Chicken curry

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 1/2 Kilo Chicken
  2. 4 Cardamom
  3. 8 Cloves
  4. 1/2 tsp Pepper
  5. 1 tsp Cumin
  6. 2 tbsp Coriander seeds
  7. 2 inches Cinnamon
  8. 1/3 cup Dry Coconut
  9. Stone Flower -Little
  10. 1 tbsp Poppy seeds
  11. 1 Kapok buds
  12. 15 Red Chilli
  13. 1/2 cup Curd
  14. 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  15. 1/4 tsp Turmeric
  16. Salt - as per taste
  17. 2 tbsp Oil
  18. 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  19. 4 tbsp Slit Green Chillies
  20. Chopped Coriander and Mint Leaves- little
  21. For the Curry-
  22. 1/4 cup Oil
  23. 1/2 cup Chopped Onions
  24. 10 Garlic
  25. 2 Strands Curry Leaves
  26. 1 cup Chopped Tomatoes
  27. 1/2 liter Water
  28. 1 tsp Lemon juice
  29. Chopped Coriander leaves -little
INSTRUCTIONS: Roast all the ingredients used for Chicken masala on a low flame till you get a nice smell. Put it in a mixie jar. Add Curd, Salt, Ginger Garlic paste, Turmeric and grind into a smooth paste adding water. Marinate the Chicken pieces, adding the masala paste, Oil, Lemon juice, Chopped Green Chillies, Coriander and Pudina. Mix thoroughly and leave it for at least 30 minutes. Heat some Oil in a pan. Fry Chopped Onions, Garlic and Curry leaves, till they turn Golden brown. Add Tomato pieces to it and cook till the skin of Tomatoes get separated. Put the marinated Chicken pieces into this gravy and cook on high flame till the oil floats to the top, keep stirring occasionally Then add 500ml water and cook on a medium flame till the oil floats to the top. It may roughly take upto 25 minutes. Add Lemon Juice and Garnish it with Chopped Coriander and remove it from the fire. Serve it with hot Rice, Jowar Roti's or Fried Rice.
