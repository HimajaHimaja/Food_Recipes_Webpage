Carrot ginger soup

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 300 gm Carrot
  2. 800 ml Water
  3. 1 Bay Leaf
  4. 5 Cloves Garlic
  5. 1/2 tbsp Pepper corns
  6. 1/2 Inch Ginger
  7. Salt (to taste)
  8. 1 tbsp Oil
  9. 1/4 Cup Onion (Diced)
INSTRUCTIONS:Heat the oil in a pan and fry Bay leaf, Garlic, Pepper Corns, and Ginger pieces for 2-3 minutes. Put in the onion and carrot pieces and fry for 3 more minutes. Then add Â½ litre water and let it cook on medium flame till soft. Grind the softly boiled Carrot mixture into a smooth paste along with the water. Put the Carrot paste through a sieve to remove any hard pieces or fibre. Add 300 ml water and boil on high flame. As the soup boils, you will notice that foam floats on top. Carefully scoop away the foam. Add Salt and take off the fire. Serve piping hot.
