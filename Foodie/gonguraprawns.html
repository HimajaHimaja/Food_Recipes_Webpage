Gongura Prawns

INGREDIENTS:
    For Frying the Prawns-
    1. 250 gms Prawns
    2. 2 tbsp Oil
    3. ¼ tsp Turmeric
    4. Salt – A little
    For the Curry-
    1. 4 tbsp Oil
    2. 1 inch Cinnamon stick (Dalchini)
    3. 4 Cloves (Laung)
    4. 4 Cardamom (Ilaichi)
    5. ½ Bay Leaf (Tez Patta)
    6. ¼ tsp Cumin (Zeera)
    7. 3 sprigs Curry Leaves
    8. 3 slit Green Chillies ((lengthwise))
    9. ½ cup Onion (Sliced)
    10. Salt – To taste
    11. ½ tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
    12. ½ cup Tomato (Chopped)
    13. ¼ tsp Turmeric
    14. 1 ½ tsp Chilli Powder
    15. 1 tsp Roasted Dhaniya Powder
    16. 1 tsp Roasted Zeera Powder
    17. 4 tbsp Water
    18. 250 gms Gongura Leaves(sorreal leaves)
    19. ½ cup Water
    20. ½ tbsp Garam Masala
    21. 1 tbsp Green Chillies chopped
    22. 2 sprigs Curry Leaves
INSTRUCTIONS: Heat oil. Add the cleaned prawns, Salt and Turmeric, and fry till the water from the Prawns dries up. Keep the Prawns aside. Heat the oil for the curry gravy and add all the masala ingredients, Zeera, Curry Leaves, and Green chillies. Fry well. In the roasted masalas, add chopped onions and salt, and fry till onions are golden brown. Now add Ginger-Garlic paste and fry well. Once it is fried, add Chopped Tomatoes and fry till tomatoes turn into a mish-mash. At this point, add Chilli Powder, Turmeric, Dhaniya Powder, Zeera powder and some water. Fry well, taking care to see that the masala does not burn. Fry till il floats on top. Add Gongura leaves and cook till the leaves get pulpy. Now add the prawns and some water. Cook till oil floats on top. Sprinkle a little Garam Masala, Finely chopped Green Chillies, and Curry Leaves. Take off the heat. The dish is ready to be served.
