Butter Naan

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 teaspoon sugar
  3. 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  6. 2 tablespoons melted butter
  7. 1/2 cup warm milk
  8. Butter for brushing
INSTRUCTIONS: Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Step 2: Add the plain yogurt and melted butter to the bowl. Mix well. Step 3: Gradually add the warm milk to the mixture and knead until a soft dough forms. Step 4: Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 1 hour. Step 5: After the resting time, divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a round shape using a rolling pin. Step 6: Heat a non-stick pan or tawa over medium heat. Step 7: Place the rolled naan on the hot pan and cook for about 1 minute or until bubbles start to appear. Step 8: Flip the naan and cook for another minute, pressing gently with a spatula to ensure it cooks evenly. Step 9: Remove the naan from the pan and brush it with butter. Step 10: Repeat the process with the remaining dough portions. Step 11: Serve the butter naan warm and enjoy it with your favorite curries or dips!
