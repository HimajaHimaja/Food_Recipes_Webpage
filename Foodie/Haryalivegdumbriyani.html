Haryali Veg Dum Biryani

Dry spices
  1. 1/2 tsp Turmeirc

  4. 1.5 tsp Mirchi Powder
  5. 1/2 tsp Pepper Powder
  6. 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  7. 1 tsp Roasted Cumin Powder
  8. 1/2 cup Peas
  9. 1/2 cup Curd
  10. 1 tsp Lemon Juice
To cook Biryani Rice-
  1. 2 liter Water
  2. 2 Green Chillies
  3. 2.5 tbsp Salt
  4. 5 Cloves
  5. 1 Biryani leaf
  6. 1 tbsp Black Cumin
  7. 6 Cardamoms
  8. 1 Kapok bud
  9. 1 Nutmeg
  10. 1.5 Cinnamon
  11. 2 cups Soaked Basmati Rice (370 gm)
  12. 1 tsp Kesar food colour
  13. 1/4 tsp Garam Masala
  14. 2 tsp Ghee
INSTRUCTIONS:Add all the ingredients for the Green Paste and make a smooth paste with cool water. Add Ghee and Oil to the vessel and heat it. Add the dry Biryani spices and roast. In the spices, add the Onions and fry until they turn soft. Then add the rest of the vegetables and fry till they are cooked to 60%. In the fried vegetables, add Salt and the rest of the dry masalas and then switch off the stove. After the stove is switched off, add the Green Paste, frozen Peas, Curd, Lime juice, a tbsp Ghee and Oil and mix well. To cook Biryani Rice, add all the spices and Salt in boiling Water and boil for another 3-4 minutes. In the boiling Water, add the soaked Rice and cook to 80 % in a high flame. Strain the 80% cooked Rice and spread it on the vegetables coated with spices. After spreading the Rice, add some Garam Masala and Ghee and add water from the cooked Biryani Rice and spread it all over the Rice. Add some Orange food colour as well. Seal the edges of the vessel with Maida paste, leaving one spot from where steam can escape. Cover the vessel so that the Dum does not go out. On high flame, the steam comes out of the spot left open. Then reduce the flame and cook for 8 minutes. Then switch off the stove and let it rest for 20 minutes. Then uncover the aromatic Green Biryani and serve with cool Raita.
