Dragon Chicken

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 500g boneless chicken, cut into strips
  2. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  3. 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  4. 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  5. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  7. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  8. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  9. 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  10. Oil for frying
INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. 1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken with soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, black pepper powder,
    and salt. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. 2. After marinating, add cornstarch to the chicken and mix well to coat each piece.
  3. 3. Heat oil in a pan or wok for deep frying. Once the oil is hot, add the coated chicken pieces and fry until they turn golden brown and
    crispy.
  4. 4. Remove the fried chicken from the oil and place it on a paper towel to remove excess oil.
  5. 5. Serve hot with your favorite sauce or as an appetizer.
