Hotandsour soup

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  2. 1 cup mushrooms, thinly sliced
  3. 1/2 cup bamboo shoots, julienned
  4. 1/2 cup tofu, cubed
  5. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  6. 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  7. 1 tablespoon cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons water
  8. 1 egg, beaten
  9. 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  10. 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  11. Salt and pepper to taste
  12. Chopped green onions for garnishing
INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a boil.
  2. Add the mushrooms, bamboo shoots, and tofu to the pot. Let them simmer for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce and rice vinegar. Pour the mixture into the pot and stir well.
  4. In a separate bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in water to create a slurry. Slowly pour the slurry into the soup while stirring continuously. This will help thicken the soup.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 5 minutes.
  6. Slowly drizzle the beaten egg into the soup while stirring gently. This will create beautiful egg ribbons in the soup.
  7. Add the sesame oil and chili flakes to the soup. Stir well and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cook the soup for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through.
  9. Serve the hot and sour soup hot, garnished with chopped green onions.
