Laccha Parata

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 teaspoon sugar
  3. 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  6. 2 tablespoons melted butter
  7. 1/4 cup warm milk
  8. 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  9. Butter for brushing
INSTRUCTIONS:
  • Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • Step 2: Add the plain yogurt and melted butter to the bowl. Mix well.
  • Step 3: Gradually add the warm milk to the mixture and knead until a soft dough forms.
  • Step 4: Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 1 hour.
  • Step 5: After the resting time, divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a round shape using a rolling pin.
  • Step 6: Brush melted butter on the rolled dough and sprinkle chopped cilantro on top.
  • Step 7: Starting from one end, make a series of pleats or folds in the dough, like you're folding a fan.
  • Step 8: Once you reach the other end, roll the folded dough into a spiral shape.
  • Step 9: Gently press down on the spiral to flatten it slightly.
  • Step 10: Heat a non-stick pan or tawa over medium heat.
  • Step 11: Place the rolled laccha naan on the hot pan and cook for about 1-2 minutes on each side, or until golden brown spots appear.
  • Step 12: Remove the naan from the pan and brush it with butter.
  • Step 13: Repeat the process with the remaining dough portions.
  • Step 14: Serve the parato naan warm and enjoy it with your favorite curries or dips!
