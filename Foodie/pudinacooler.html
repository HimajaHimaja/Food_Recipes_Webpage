Pudina Cooler

INGREDIENTS:
  1. Mint Leaves – One bunch
  2. Green Coriander – One small bunch
  3. 3/4 Inch Ginger
  4. 4 Cardamoms
  5. 50 - 60 gms Jaggery
  6. 750 ml Water
  7. 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
INSTRUCTIONS:Pour Water into Jaggery and let it melt.
Make a smooth paste of the rest of the ingredients.
Strain the melted Jaggery and add the Mint paste to it. Mix well.
