Kivi

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 2 Kiwi
  2. 3 Thinly sliced Lemons
  3. 2 tbsp Sugar
  4. Sprite/Cool Soda
  5. 7 - 8 Ice Cubes
INSTRUCTIONS:Crush Kiwi pieces, thinly sliced Lemons and Ice in a mortar or a tall glass. After the mixture is crushed more than a half, add Sugar and crush to 80%. Fill a glass to 1/3rd with Kiwi Crush and add Ice Cubes. Pour Sprite over it to fill the glass.
