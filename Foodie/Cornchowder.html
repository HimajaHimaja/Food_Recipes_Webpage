Corn Chowder

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 4 cups corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  2. 1 onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  4. 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  5. 4 slices of bacon, chopped
  6. 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  7. 1 cup milk
  8. 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  9. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  10. 2 tablespoons butter
  11. Salt and pepper to taste
  12. Chopped fresh parsley for garnishing
INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. 1. In a large pot, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon and set it aside, leaving the bacon fat in the pot.
  2. 2. Add the chopped onion to the pot and cook until it becomes translucent.
  3. 3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. 4. Add the diced potatoes and corn kernels to the pot. Stir everything together.
  5. 5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and butter until it forms a smooth paste.
  6. 6. Add the flour-butter mixture to the pot and stir well to coat the vegetables.
  7. 7. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  8. 8. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
  9. 9. Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, blend about half of the soup until creamy.
    This step is optional if you prefer a chunkier texture.
  10. 10. Stir in the milk and heavy cream (if using) into the soup. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. 11. Cook the soup for an additional 5 minutes to heat through.
  12. 12. Serve the corn chowder soup hot, garnished with the crispy bacon and chopped fresh parsley.
