Mutton soup

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  2. 1 onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 carrots, diced
  4. 2 potatoes, diced
  5. 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  6. 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  7. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  8. 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  9. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Freshly ground black pepper
  12. Chopped fresh cilantro for garnish
INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. In a large pot, heat some oil and saut√© the onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic and ginger paste, and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the mutton pieces to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Add the turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  5. Pour in enough water to cover the mutton and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 1-2 hours until the mutton is tender.
  6. Add the diced carrots and potatoes to the pot and cook until they are soft.
  7. Adjust the seasoning if needed and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your flavorful mutton soup!
