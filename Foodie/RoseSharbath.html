Rose Sharbath

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 100 ml Rose Syrup
  2. 2 tbsps Chia Seeds
  3. 1.25 tbsp Lemon Juice
  4. 8 - 10 Ice Cubes
  5. 600 ml Cold Water
INSTRUCTIONS:Soak Chia Seeds until they get swollen.
Add the rest of the ingredients - Rose Syrup, Ice, Cold Water and Lemon Juice to the seeds,
Mix well and serve.
