Chicken Vindaloo-Nonveg

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  2. 2 onions, finely chopped
  3. 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  4. 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  5. 2-3 green chilies, slit
  6. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  7. 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  8. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  9. 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  10. 1 tablespoon vinegar
  11. 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  12. Salt to taste
  13. Oil for cooking
  14. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
INSTRUCTIONS:1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter. 2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. 3. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute. 4. In a small bowl, mix together turmeric powder, red chili powder, vinegar, and tomato paste to make a paste. 5. Add the paste to the pan and cook for a couple of minutes. 6. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and mix well, ensuring they are coated with the spice mixture. 7. Season with salt and cook on medium heat until the chicken is cooked through and tender. 8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
