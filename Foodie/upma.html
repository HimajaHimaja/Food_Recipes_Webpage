Upma

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 1 cup Semolina (Bombay Rava)
  2. 2 Diced Tomatoes
  3. 1/4 cup Grated Carrot
  4. 1/2 cup Chopped Onions
  5. 1/4 cup Fresh Green Peas
  6. 1 Green Chillies
  7. 1 tbsp Chopped Ginger
  8. 1 Curry leaves
  9. 2 tbsps Chopped Green Coriander
  10. Cashew Nuts
  11. 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  12. 1 tsp Bengal Gram
  13. 1 tsp Black Gram
  14. 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  15. 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  16. Salt
  17. 2 tbsps Oil
  18. 1/4 cup Ghee
  19. 3 cups Water
INSTRUCTIONS:Roast the Semolina on low flame until it gives out a nice aroma. Tomato Baath is soft only if you roast it on low flame. Heat 1 tbsp Ghee, 2 tbsp Oil and roast Cashew Nuts until they turn golden.
Add Mustard Seeds, Cumin, Black Gram, Bengal
Gram to the same oil, fry well, add Chopped Onions and fry for another 2 minutes. Then add Green Chillies and chopped Ginger and fry until onions turn soft.
Add diced tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes. And then add grated Carrot, Fresh Green Peas, Turmeric, Curry leaves and Salt and cook to about 80 percent.
Now add water and bring it to a boil. Add Semolina, mix well, cover it and cook for 3 minutes.
Add Green Coriander, Cashew Nuts, 2 tbsps Ghee. Mix well, take it off the fire and serve.
