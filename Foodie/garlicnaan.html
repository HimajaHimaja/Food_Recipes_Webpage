Garlic Naan

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 teaspoon sugar
  3. 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  6. 2 tablespoons melted butter
  7. 1/2 cup warm milk
  8. 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  9. Butter for brushing
  10. Chopped cilantro (optional)
INSTRUCTIONS:
  • Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • Step 2: Add the plain yogurt and melted butter to the bowl. Mix well.
  • Step 3: Gradually add the warm milk to the mixture and knead until a soft dough forms.
  • Step 4: Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 1 hour.
  • Step 5: After the resting time, divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a round shape using a rolling pin.
  • Step 6: Sprinkle minced garlic on one side of the rolled naan and press it gently into the dough.
  • Step 7: Heat a non-stick pan or tawa over medium heat.
  • Step 8: Place the rolled naan with the garlic side down on the hot pan and cook for about 1 minute or until bubbles start to appear.
  • Step 9: Flip the naan and cook for another minute, pressing gently with a spatula to ensure it cooks evenly.
  • Step 10: Remove the naan from the pan and brush it with butter.
  • Step 11: Optional: Sprinkle chopped cilantro on top for added flavor.
  • Step 12: Repeat the process with the remaining dough portions.
  • Step 13: Serve the garlic naan warm and enjoy it with your favorite curries or dips!
