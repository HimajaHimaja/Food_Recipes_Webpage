Fruit custord

INGREDIENTS:
  2. 1 liter Milk
  3. 4 tbsp Vanilla flavor Custard Powder
  4. 1/2 cup Sugar
  5. 1/4 tsp Mixed Fruit Essence
  6. 1 Banana
  7. 1 Apple
  8. 1 Sapota ( Chiku )
  9. 1 Kiwi
  10. 6 - 7 Fresh Dates
  11. 50 gms Cashew Nuts
  12. 50 gms Black Grapes
  13. 50 gms Green Grapes
  14. 1/2 cup Musk Melon slices
  15. 1/2 cup Pomegranate seeds
INSTRUCTIONS:Add Custard Powder and Essence to 1/4th liter of Milk and mix so that there are no lumps. Boil some Milk once and add Sugar and the Custard Milk. Let it become thick without lumps like Buttermilk and keep it in the fridge for two hours. Two hours later, the Custard becomes hard like Curd. Then put it in a mixie and blend until it becomes creamy.
