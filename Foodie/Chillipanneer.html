Chilli Panner

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 200 grams paneer, cut into cubes
  2. 1 onion, finely chopped
  3. 1 capsicum (bell pepper), thinly sliced
  4. 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  5. 2 tablespoons oil
  6. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  7. 2 tablespoons tomato ketchup
  8. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  9. 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  10. 1 teaspoon vinegar
  11. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  12. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  13. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  14. Salt to taste
  15. Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
INSTRUCTIONS:Step 1: Heat oil in a pan or kadai over medium heat. Step 2: Add the finely chopped onion and sauté until it turns translucent. Step 3: Add the ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute until fragrant. Step 4: Add the thinly sliced capsicum and cook for a couple of minutes until it becomes slightly tender. Step 5: Now, add the tomato ketchup, soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar. Mix well. Step 6: Add the red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Stir everything together. Step 7: Gently place the paneer cubes into the pan and coat them with the sauce mixture. Step 8: Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the paneer is well-coated with the flavors. Step 9: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and give it a final mix. Step 10: Serve the delicious chili paneer hot as an appetizer or as a side dish with roti, naan, or rice.
