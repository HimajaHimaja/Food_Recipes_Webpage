Pepper Chicken

INGREDIENTS:
  1. 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  2. 1 onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 tomatoes, pureed
  4. 2 tablespoons oil
  5. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  7. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  8. 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
  9. Salt to taste
  10. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the chopped onions until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned.
  4. Now, add the tomato puree, black pepper powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Cover the pan and let the chicken cook on medium heat for about 15-20 minutes or until it is cooked through.
  6. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  7. Once the chicken is cooked and the gravy has thickened, garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.
